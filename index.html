Is ARB Pay Safe or Fake?

Many users consider ARB Pay a legitimate platform. However, scammers do impersonate support. Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or password with anyone.

Signs of a Legitimate App

  • Fast transactions with high order availability
  • Clear terms shown before every transaction
  • No direct holding of user funds (C2C model)
  • Support only through official in-app live chat

Warning Signs of a Scam

✕Anyone asking for OTP or password via phone or Telegram
✕Login links sent through unknown messages
✕Websites with different URLs claiming to be ARB Pay
✕Anyone contacting you first claiming to be ARB Pay support
💡 To test a suspicious site: enter a wrong password — if it still logs you in, it is a phishing site.
⚠️ ARB Pay is not regulated by RBI. Use at your own risk and start with small amounts only.
