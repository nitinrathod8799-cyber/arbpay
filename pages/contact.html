How to Submit a Support Ticket
1
Open the ARB Pay app on your device.
2
Go to the Support or Help section from the menu.
3
Describe your issue clearly in the chat box.
4
Attach a screenshot if your issue involves a transaction.
5
Submit and wait — a support agent will respond within the app.
💡 For transaction disputes, always click Appeal inside the order — do not wait for support to resolve it manually.
⚠️ Scam Warning — Fake Support
✕Anyone calling or messaging you claiming to be "ARB Pay Support" is a scammer.
✕Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or account password with anyone for any reason.
✕Fake Telegram groups may impersonate ARB Pay. Only use the official link above.
✕Phishing websites may look identical to ARB Pay. Always verify the URL is arbpay.me before logging in.
