ARB Pay Kya Hai?

ARB Pay ek C2C (Customer-to-Customer) platform hai jahan aap ARB tokens buy aur sell kar sakte ho. Payment directly UPI se hoti hai — Google Pay, PhonePe, ya koi bhi UPI app use kar sakte ho.

Official website hai arbpay.me aur app ka naam hai ARB Wallet. Social media pe ise AR Wallet bhi kehte hain.

💡 ARB Pay aapke paise hold nahi karta. Buyer aur seller directly UPI se payment karte hain.
