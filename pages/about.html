What is ARB Pay?

ARB Pay — also known as ARB Wallet or AR Wallet — is a Customer-to-Customer (C2C) digital trading platform where users buy and sell ARB tokens. The platform is directly UPI-integrated, so payments arrive instantly without delays.

Its official domain is arbpay.me and the app name is ARB Wallet, but on YouTube, Telegram, and social media it is widely known as AR Wallet.

💡 ARB Pay does not hold any user funds. Payments happen between buyer and seller via UPI only — the platform only matches the two parties.
