Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here is how this website handles your information.

🔐 Last updated: January 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how arbpay.us collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our site. We are a guide website — we do not operate the ARB Pay app, process any payments, or store your trading or financial data.

What We Collect

This website does not require you to create an account or submit any personal information. However, limited data may be collected automatically: IP address (server logs), browser and device type (compatibility), and pages visited (analytics only).

We do not collect: your name or email, payment or UPI information, or ARB Pay login data.

How We Use Information

Any automatically collected data is used solely for improving website performance, understanding which guides are most helpful, and detecting and preventing spam or malicious traffic. We do not sell, rent, or share your browsing data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

This website may use basic browser cookies to improve your experience. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

💡 External links (like arbpay.me or Telegram) have their own cookie and privacy policies that we do not control.

Security

We take reasonable steps to keep this website secure. We recommend: never enter your ARB Pay password on any site other than arbpay.me, never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone, and use a secure internet connection when accessing financial platforms.

Children's Privacy

This website is not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect any information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of this website after any changes means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
